Gas Leak
Useful Information

  1. Refer to SOP’s, pre-incident plans.
  2. Refer to city/town evacuation procedures if situation warrants.
  3. Determine wind direction and speed.
  4. Determine weather forecast – effects of rain on gas cloud.
  5. Consider dangers to traffic flow.
  6. Consider proximity to population centers, institutions, hospitals, nursing homes, etc.
  7. Establish separate phone number(s) for media enquiries, (NOT in the communication center).
