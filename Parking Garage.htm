TR Knowledgebase: Parking Garage Fire

Parking Garage Fire
Useful Information

Possible trapped persons, especially below ground

  • Consider the effects of flammable liquid runoff into drains.
  • Usual fire fighting apparatus may not be able to enter garage due to low ceiling.
  • Firefighting on upper levels may need specialist equipment.
  • If parking garage is connected to mall or other building, consider the effects of smoke or fire spread and possible evacuation
  • BEWARE of potential explosion risk from vehicles in the garage!
