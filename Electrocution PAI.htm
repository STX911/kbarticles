Electrocution
Pre-Arrival Advice

If the victim is still in contact with the electrical source, rescue should only be attempted by those skilled in this type of situation.

TURN OFF THE POWER, IF POSSIBLE, AND IT IS SAFE TO DO SO!

  • If in the home: Turn off the main switch
  • If utility wires: Can you safely get the nearest pole number?

If high voltage electrocution: DO NOT enter the area or attempt to remove any wires or other material around the victim!

If the voltage is high enough, everything can conduct the electricity!
