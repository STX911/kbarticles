TR Knowledgebase: High Rise Fire

High Rise Fire
Useful Information

This situation has a potential for a high life threat with mass casualties.

  • Multiple alarms and mutual aid may be necessary.
  • Helicopter rescues from the roof may be necessary.
  • Evacuation from the immediate locality may be necessary.
  • Depending on which floor(s) are involved, instructions for evacuation may differ.
