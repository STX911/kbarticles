Active Shooter
Useful Information

Law Enforcement’s primary goal is to locate, contain and stop the offender.  The key to a successful Law Enforcement response:

·         Knowledge of assailant(s) location and weapon(s).

 

Assailant(s) Information

·         Location:  Seek specific Location within Location [building name or number, room number, etc.]

·         Identity:  Name(s), if known, description(s), special abilities or skills, body armor, backpacks, etc.

·         Weapons / Explosives:  type, location, number.

 

Critical Actions

·         Access any available floor plans.

·         Determine if there are other hazards/dangers present.

·         Attempt to determine the safest approach, points of egress, elevators, stairwells.

·         Activate the SWAT or ERT, if appropriate.                                                                                                               

·         Activate Mutual Aid Agreements, if appropriate                                                                                                            

·         Initiate the Incident Command system.  Remember to use plain language and avoid acronyms.

·         Activate any other Emergency Operations Plans and PIO response, if appropriate.

 

Victims/Callers Information

·         Location:   Seek specific Location within Location [building name or number, room number, etc.]  Is this a place of safety?  Provide options to caller to deter, delay, or deny access by the assailant(s

·         Injuries:  Number and type of injuries

·         Identity:  Name(s), if known, description(s)
