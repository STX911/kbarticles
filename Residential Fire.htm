TR Knowledgebase: Residential Fire

Residential Fire
Useful Information

Consider time of day and likely number of occupants

  • Is it possible there could be any mentally or physically handicapped occupants.
  • Have there been any previous calls to this address? If so, is there a pattern and previous damage?
  • Break inverted communications: Ask "As I look at the house from the street, where is the fire / where are the persons trapped?"
