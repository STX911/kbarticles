TR Knowledgebase: Psychiatric

Psychiatric / Behavioral Problems
Useful Information

A calm approach and helping attitude are essential. Alert crisis intervention, suicide prevention services and/or police as necessary.

If a patient must be restrained, this is left to law enforcement and appropriately trained EMS personnel.

A silent approach to the scene (no red lights or sirens) may be indicated.

A psychiatric like emergency may actually be an emotional response to illness, injury or stress in a person who does not suffer from a psychiatric disorder.
