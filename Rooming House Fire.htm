TR Knowledgebase: Rooming House Fire

Rooming House Fire
Useful Information

Consider time of day and likely number of occupants

  • Ask for a list of occupants if possible.
  • Consider the possibility of mentally and/or physically handicapped residents.
  • Incident may require multiple alarms due to potential life hazard.
  • Have there been previous calls to this location? Is there a pattern?
  • The fire protection standards of these building can often be out of date.
  • Many rooming homes do not have room numbers marked. The best way to identify the room may be by floor location (i.e., "2nd floor, 3rd door on the left")
