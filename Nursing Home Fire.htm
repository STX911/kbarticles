TR Knowledgebase: Nursing Home Fire

Nursing Home Fire
Useful Information

Attempt to obtain roster or number and names of patients.

  • Potential multiple alarm may be necessary due to life threat.
  • Potential evacuation – consider where to send victims. Local disaster plans may need to be activated to arrange sheltering of non-ambulatory patients.
  • Evacuation may require multiple EMS units
  • Potential for confused patients to attempt to re-enter building and physically disabled may need to be rescued.
