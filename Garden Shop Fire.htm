TR Knowledgebase: Garden Shop Fire

Garden Shop / Plant Fire
Useful Information

This incident may need to be upgraded to HAZMAT incident.

  • Beware of potentially hazardous fumes.
  • Consider the need for evacuation of the locality around the incident if fumes threaten to spread.
  • Water runoff from this type of fire can contaminate water bodies with residue from hazardous materials and pesticides. State and local environmental protection agencies may need to be advised.
  • Be alert to the need to activate a HAZMAT team, consider using HAZARDOUS MATERIAL procedure.
