TR Knowledgebase: Hospital Fire

Hospital Fire
Useful Information

This situation has a potential for a high life threat with mass casualties.

  • Potential evacuation – consider where to send victims. Local disaster plans may have to be activated to arrange sheltering of non-ambulatory patients.
  • Consider the potential threat from medical gases, electrical problems and biohazards.
  • If confirmed fire, ensure local EMS agency is prepared for evacuation / transfer of patients.
  • Advise supervisor so a separate phone number can be published for media inquiries, NOT IN THE COMMUNICATIONS CENTER.
