TR Knowledgebase: School Fire

School Fire
Useful Information

Consider time of day and likely number of occupants

  • Evacuation from upper floor may be necessary and the potential for a mass casualty incident may exist.
  • In extreme weather conditions consider the provision of shelter from the elements for those evacuated.
  • The potential for hazard threat may exist from sources such as, pool chemicals, chemistry labs and asbestos (in older structures).
  • Anticipate significant volume of parental, public and media inquiries which may require a separate information line being provided outside the communications center.
